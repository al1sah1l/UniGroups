We visited Startup Bootcamp in Kyiv



On October 30-31, we attended an incredibly cool event "Science & Business Startup Bootcamp".

During these two days, we had the opportunity to listen to really useful speeches by speakers on important topics for startups, to talk to interesting people. We have written down a lot of important points about product launching, legal litigation, patenting, and grants, which we will definitely use in the future.

In addition, we consulted with the director of the Ukrainian Startup Fund, Pavel Kartashov, on how to apply for a grand and be competitive. We met and talked with Emanuel Volpe, Director of Innovation at DTech, and Volodymyr Bugrov, Rector of the Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv.



At the end of the event, we were given the opportunity to perform a pitch about our system. We took the opportunity and were marked as one of the participants with the best product presentation. After receiving advice from experts and event organizers, we also received small gifts from a DTech partner and certificates of participation.



We would like to thank the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine, the Ukrainian Startup Fund, and the Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv for organizing such a cool event. We hope to meet again in a month, where we will get even more interesting information and have a pitch a thousand times better!
