Analysis of the EdTech Market: what systems are used in different countries?



Do you think there are many universities in Ukraine that use systems for organizing education in Ukraine? Or around the world? We had only a general idea of ​​this before we analyzed the market in detail. And now we want to share with you some of the results of our research.

At the moment, we are actively working on an application to the Ukrainian Startup Fund for a grant. We did most of the work - the next item was market analysis and compiling a table of competitors. That`s what allowed us to fully realize where we are going, with whom we compete, and build a development plan.

To analyze the market, we used several sources of information: we communicated with students and university employees, used ready-made research from the Internet, and searched for information on the websites of universities. Thus, we got a clear and broad analysis of which systems and in which universities are used.

As a result, it should be noted that the market is relatively free to promote commercial products. Most universities in Ukraine use either Moodle, which is a free open source solution. Or they develop their own systems, which most often have many problems with configuration and support.

In Europe, Canvas and Selma are very common systems. But most of the universities still prefer Moodle. Speaking about the countries of North America, namely the USA and Canada, there are Canvas, Blackboard, Brightspace are leading in the market, but they also have their own systems.

Thus, we can say that the existing systems are far from ideal - each has its own strengths and weaknesses. Our goal is to realize what they do not have and to become the most demanded system for organizing training in all universities in Ukraine and around the world!
